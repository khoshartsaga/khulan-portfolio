Netflix

Overview

Redesigned in 2014, the N is a “simple and iconic” trademark of Netflix. It represents connection to a endless stream of stories.

Enjoying Flicks with Friends & Family on Netflix

Watching video streams together online has been gaining popularity, with many third-party applications and video streaming services offering this capability, but this key feature has been seemingly absent in Netflix. How can we help provide meaningful virtual experiences for people to connect with each other while watching on Netflix? I led and created the design integration of this feature so that users may feel connected while watching videos on Netflix.

My Role

Lead UX Designer, UX Researcher, Prototyper

Village Team Size

3

Project Duration

2 weeks

This is an academic and conceptual project designed by me and my teammates. Though it would be nice to work for Netflix, this project is not affiliated with them in any manner. The tools I used in this project were Figma, Google Suite, Lucidchart, and Trello.

The (Ap-pear-ent) Problem

Since July 2021, the iconic video streaming service, Netflix, has had over 209 million global paid memberships.

Netflix’s brand is iconic and their site has a streamlined user flow. However, they have missed an opportunity to help enhance their user’s collective experience by opting not to provide a feature for users to watch video streams together. The goal is to integrate this feature without interfering with Netflix’s streamlined user flow and its iconic branding.

Let’s Craft

Understanding

In order to develop and craft this new feature on Netflix, I needed to gain a better understanding of people’s current behaviors, needs, wants, and frustrations through user research.

To All the Netflix Users I’ve Listened to Before

  • Users want a shared video streaming experience feature within Netflix rather than a browser extension or a third-party application.
  • Users need it to be intuitive, easy to use, and navigable.
  • Users are frustrated with current third-party video stream sharing services due to issues relating to connectivity or features.

Designing

I chose to integrate the feature as seamlessly as possible by reusing existing components from Netflix as well as trying my best to adhere to their brand guidelines. This can potentially provide a sense of familiarity for Netflix users when they are navigating this new feature.

Note: A split-screen layout was utilized so that users can interact with friends & family without sacrificing content

Crafted Solution

Putting the pieces together, our team was able to craft a meaningful and enjoyable experience for Netflix users who want to connect with others through their favorite movies and shows.

NOOK Rewards

It was really fun and surprisingly educational to work on crafting and designing a concept feature for Netflix.

Creating Netflix Party made me take into consideration a lot of Netflix’s current brand and how I can build the new feature on top of that. It was interesting to see how much thought and consideration went into the creation of Netflix and I tried to emulate that within my designs. This project also pushed me to try and build purposeful interactions. It was warming to see my friends’ faces brighten when they were able to interact with the prototype.
