IN CREATIVE CO

Overview

Designed by In Creative Company’s apprentice graphic designer, Samantha Le, this graphic represents the diverse community of womxn and non-binary creatives who work with In Creative Co.

Empowering a Community of Freelance Creatives

In Creative Company was created to help freelancing creatives find work for their services and a supportive community of fellow creatives. Born out of COVID-19, they are a creative agency and collective hybrid that is rooted in collaboration, togetherness, and creativity.

For this project, I led the design direction to craft a better meaningful experience for current members of In Creative Company. This encompassed creating wireframes, prototypes, communicating design decisions to the client, listening to client needs, and adhering to the company’s clear-cut brand guidelines.

Currently, I am freelancing with In Creative Company as a UX designer to complete their website redesign. Stay tuned for updates!
